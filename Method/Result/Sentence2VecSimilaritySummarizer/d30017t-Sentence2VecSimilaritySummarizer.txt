Summary for topic d30017t:
A congressman who visited remote parts of North Korea last week said  Saturday that the food and health situation there was desperate and  deteriorating, and that millions of North Koreans might have starved  to death in the last few years. Citing a North Korean human rights group and Japanese tourists  visiting the region, South Korea's Yonhap News Agency reported Monday  that 150 North Koreans had been sent home from China, despite having  presented petitions for political asylum. ``A  lot of the children were too weak to come to school,'' he said. Virtually all the children under 7 who  are malnourished also have diarrhea, he said.
