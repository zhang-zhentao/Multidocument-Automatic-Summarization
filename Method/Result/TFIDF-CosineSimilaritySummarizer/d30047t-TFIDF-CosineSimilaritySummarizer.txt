Summary for topic d30047t:
Zarya is  to fly alone for two weeks before a rendezvous with the American space  shuttle Endeavor, which is to be launched Dec. 3 carrying the Unity  connecting module. It will serve as a space tugboat in the  early stages of the project, providing propulsion, power and communications. The station will weigh 500 tons (more than a million pounds) and is  expected to cost at least dlrs 40 billion, with the United States  planning to pay dlrs 21 billion. It will serve as an orbital home  for visiting astronauts and cosmonauts for up to 20 years.
