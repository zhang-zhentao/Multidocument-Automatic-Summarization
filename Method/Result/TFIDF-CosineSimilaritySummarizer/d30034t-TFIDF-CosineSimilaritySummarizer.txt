Summary for topic d30034t:
Indonesia invaded East Timor  in 1975 and annexed it the following year. The United Nations does  not recognize Indonesian claims to East Timor. Representatives of exiled East Timorese pro-independence groups said  Friday that Indonesian troops attacked unarmed civilians in a village  in the disputed Southeast Asian territory, killing one East Timorese  and wounding 22 others. The attack took place Tuesday near Cailaco  in East Timor, a former Portuguese colony, according to a statement  issued by the pro-independence Christian Democratic Union of East  Timor.
