Summary for topic d30051t:
The Yugoslav war crimes tribunal cleared Zejnil Delalic,  a Muslim, of responsibility for war crimes committed against Serb  captives at a Bosnian government-run prison camp under his command. Prosecutor Grant Niemann said he would appeal Delalic's acquittal,  and asked judges to keep him in custody pending the outcome of the  appeal. All four defendants had faced  up to life imprisonment, the maximum punishment at the tribunal, which  has no death penalty.
